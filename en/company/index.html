Company information

This page explains the purpose, scope, listing policy, and partner-role boundaries behind Bridge Home Japan.

What this site is for

  • Bridge Home Japan is a lead-generation media site for foreign residents who need clearer rental search paths in Japan. It combines listing discovery, eligibility guidance, life-support content, and partner routing to reduce friction before application.

Scope and role boundaries

  • This site does not perform brokerage contracts, statutory disclosures, or lease execution. Those actions are handled by licensed partner agencies. Our role is limited to information design, eligibility clarification, and routing users to the right contact point.

Listing-data policy

  • Live inventory is expected to come from rights-cleared partner feeds. Public open data is used as supporting context only, while photos and floor plans are limited to sources with confirmed rights.

Frequently asked questions

Is this site itself a real-estate agency?

No. Bridge Home Japan is a lead-generation media site. Licensed partner agencies handle brokerage and lease execution.

Do you freely republish photos and floor plans?

No. We only intend to use rights-cleared partner feeds or legally reusable public data.
