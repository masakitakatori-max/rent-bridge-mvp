Frequently asked questions

Is the support hub only for after move-in?

No. Resident registration, phone setup, and banking are easier when the reader prepares before move-in.

How do support pages connect to rental search?

They connect guide intent to practical move-in tasks and reinforce theme hubs such as guarantor-company and low-initial-cost.

Does it help to read support content before choosing listings?

Yes. Knowing the document flow and day-one setup cost changes how the reader evaluates listings and screening conditions.
