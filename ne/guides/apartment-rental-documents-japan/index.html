Guide

1. Identity ra stay-status ko kagajat

Residence card, passport, current address ra contact detail sab bhanda pahila chahinchha. Abroad bata apply garne ho bhane arrival plan pani spashta banaunu parcha.

2. Income, kaam wa school ko proof

Payslip, offer letter, employment contract, wa school certificate le management side lai bharosa dinchha. Student ho bhane scholarship wa family support ko kura pani spashta lekhnu ramro.

3. Inquiry ra kagaj eutai banau

Move-in date, basne manchhe ko sankhya, employer ra budget inquiry ra application ma farak paryo bhane screening kathin hunchha. Suru dekhi same information dinu parcha.

Frequently asked questions

Residence card nabani sakda pani rental ko barema sodhna milchha?

Dherai jaso sodhna milchha. Passport, arrival plan ra school wa company ko expected paper pahilai pathaera pachhi aru ke kagajat chahinchha bhanera sodhna parcha.

Student ko lagi kun kagajat sab bhanda mahatwapurna hunchha?

Admission wa enrollment proof, scholarship wa remittance detail ra emergency contact ko plan sab bhanda thulo kura ho.
